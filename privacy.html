Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Information We Collect

NetScope may collect account email addresses, license records, linked Roblox user IDs, device identifiers, activation history, session metadata, and limited diagnostic events needed to provide licensing, abuse prevention, and support.

How We Use Information

  • To create accounts and manage license keys.
  • To verify active access and enforce device limits.
  • To detect suspicious activation patterns and protect paid access.
  • To provide support and improve product reliability.

Payment Information

Payment processing will be handled by a third-party payment provider when checkout is enabled. NetScope should store payment provider IDs and subscription status, not raw payment card data.

Retention

License, session, audit, and abuse-prevention records may be retained as long as needed for security, accounting, and support. You can request account review or removal where legally required.

Security

License keys are hashed before storage. Plugin access is controlled by short-lived tokens, revocation checks, device tracking, and backend feature verification.
